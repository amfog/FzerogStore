03 · Launch Roadmap

The Plan

01
// WEEK 1–2 · FOUNDATION
Build the Brand
  • Lock in the Fog Store name, logo & color palette (navy, mist blue, gold)
  • Create social accounts: TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X under @FogStore
  • Design 2–3 products with Printify or local print shop
  • Set up Shopify store (Basic plan, ~$29/mo) with Fog theme
02
// WEEK 3–4 · HYPE BUILD
Tease the Drop
  • Show behind-the-scenes of product design on stream & TikTok
  • Use your mousepad on stream — let chat ask about it organically
  • Create a "Founding Member" waitlist — first 50 get a discount code
  • Post "Fog Store coming soon" content 3x per week
03
// MONTH 2 · LAUNCH
Go Live
  • Launch stream: unbox your own products on camera with chat watching
  • Limited first drop — 50 units per product to create scarcity
  • Pin shop link in all bios; add to stream overlay as lower-third
  • Collab with 1–2 other Wild Rift streamers for cross-promotion
04
// MONTH 3–6 · GROW
Scale & Expand
  • Launch digital products (overlays, sound packs) for 100% margin revenue
  • Introduce monthly "Fog Drop" — limited edition seasonal items
  • Build email list via post-purchase flows (free with Shopify)
  • Explore affiliate program: let other streamers earn % on referrals

05 · Marketing Strategy

How To Get Customers

🔴 Live Stream Tactics

  • Use it on stream. Your mousepad, hoodie, mug — all visible on cam. Viewers will ask.
  • Stream milestones. "When we hit 100 subs, I'm dropping a new Fog item."
  • Chat alerts. When someone buys, trigger a sound + chat shoutout.
  • Unboxing streams. New drop reveals become events your community tunes in for.

📱 Social Content

  • TikTok/Reels. "Setup tour" content, unboxings, and "what I use to play Wild Rift" videos convert extremely well.
  • Twitter/X. Announce drops, post gameplay clips, hype the community.
  • Behind the scenes. Show product design process — fans love feeling involved.
  • Wild Rift hashtags. Tap into the existing game community beyond your own followers.

🤝 Community & Collabs

  • Collab drops. Partner with another Wild Rift streamer for a limited co-branded item.
  • Give merch to top supporters. They become walking ads and brand ambassadors.
  • Discord shop channel. Post deals, new drops, and exclusive member codes.

🛒 Shop Optimization

  • Shopify or Fourthwall. Fourthwall is free and made for streamers — no monthly fee.
  • Bundle deals. "The Fog Setup" bundle: mousepad + mug + banner pack.
  • Email capture. Offer 10% off first order for signup. Own your audience.
